Who We Are

More Than a Store — A Green Movement

Flora Haven is not just a store — it is a movement towards a greener and more sustainable future. We are an organic green initiative dedicated to promoting eco-friendly living by bringing nature closer to people's everyday lives.

What makes us unique is that we are also a non-governmental organization (NGO) driven by passionate individuals who believe in protecting and restoring our planet. Our members actively participate in tree plantation drives, urban gardening programs, and eco-education initiatives.

We believe change begins at home. A single plant on a windowsill can collectively create a powerful impact on the Earth. Together, we are not just growing plants — we are growing a better planet 🌍
